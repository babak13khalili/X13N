---
title: Imagination, Illusion, Reality
written: 11-11-2025
---

I realized that the reality of life is very different from the images and assumptions I had in my mind.
As time passes, I understand more and more that many of the ideas I had about my future, my life, and the events that might happen were far from realistic.

Life unfolds in a way that constantly shows me how unrealistic and somewhat fantastical my thoughts about the future were.
I used to imagine that after a certain period of time, certain things would happen; that I would do certain things, behave in certain ways. But no.

Once I actually find myself in the situations I had previously imagined, it becomes clear how mistaken those assumptions were, and how different the reality of my life and its trajectory is from what I had envisioned.
In truth, they were closer to illusions than to genuine expectations.
