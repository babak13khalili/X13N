**Sunday – 22:55**

Tonight, I broke again.
Collapsed.
Came apart.

And yet, I’m still alive.
My body remains, unchanged.
The world, with all its details, continues as it is, moving forward.
Time keeps passing.

I broke.
I screamed.

But even I couldn’t hear my own voice.
Let alone anyone else.
